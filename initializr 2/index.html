Who are we?

Crating Specialties is a full-service, custom crating company located in Kent, Washington.

We've been around since 2004—and we've never been about anything but crates. In the last 10 years, we've grown from two employees to 28; gone from independent to corporate-owned, and back again; and moved up the road from Fife to our current facilities in Kent, Wash.

Our Products

In our warehouse, you won't find any stock crates. Everything we make is built to size—no matter what size that is.

We're proud to create top-of-the-line products, including foam-padded crates, reuseable crates with hinges, and painted crates. We also use Intercept Technology products to protect against rust and corrosion.

Our Services

You won't find better service anywhere else. We go on-site to take measurements for every order—in fact, we prefer to do it that way.

Our team provides on-site labor for packing, industrial shrink wrapping, and delivery that's always free.
